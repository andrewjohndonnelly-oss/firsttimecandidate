Purpose — Your Why
The foundation. What drives you? This is the thing that will sustain you through the hard moments and attract people to your cause.
The Cautionary Tale In 1979, Senator Ted Kennedy was asked simply why he wanted to be president. He had no clear answer. It didn't just hurt that moment — it unraveled his entire campaign. Your 'why' must be visceral, clear, and true. Everything else is built on top of it.
